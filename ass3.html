On a quiet autumn evening, the streets lay draped in the soft glow of streetlampsAs she neared the old stone bridge, she spotted a curious object glinting by the water’s edge—a locket half-buried in the mud. Something about it felt familiar, as though it belonged to a memory she couldn’t quite grasp. She hesitated, then picked it up, wiping away the dirt to reveal initials engraved on the back—her own.
